# 🎨 Add your company logo and colours

⏱️ This takes about 3 minutes. Do it once.

Make NoticeBoard look like *your* company everywhere — on boards, on the "Loading…" screen, and on the "Board not available" screen.

## What you'll change

1. Your logo (the picture).
2. Three main colours (primary, secondary, accent).
3. Light or dark theme.
4. The font.
5. The text shown when a board is loading or unavailable.

You don't need all of these — change whichever ones matter to you.

---

## Step-by-step

### 1. Go to Settings

Click **Settings** on the left menu (or your name in the top right → **Settings**).

You'll see tabs along the top: **🎨 Brand · 📺 Display Screens · 🔤 Fonts · 🔗 Data Sources · 👥 Users**.

---

## 🎨 Brand tab

### Upload your logo

- Click **📤 Upload logo**.
- Pick a PNG or SVG file.
- Wait a second — your logo appears in the preview box.

> 💡 **No logo file?** Just leave it blank. NoticeBoard will use the first letter of your company name in a coloured circle. Looks tidy.

### Pick your colours

You have three colours to set:

- **Primary** — the main "you" colour. Used on buttons, headers, the loading spinner.
- **Secondary** — a darker partner colour. Used in gradients and accents.
- **Accent** — a "look at me" colour. Used for warnings and highlights.

Two ways to pick each one:

- Click the **coloured square** — a colour wheel pops up. Pick a colour.
- **Or** type a hex code like `#FF6600` in the box next to it. (Your designer probably knows yours.)

### Light or dark theme

A simple dropdown. Pick whichever looks better on your TVs.

> 💡 For most factory floors and warehouses, **🌙 Dark** looks cleaner from a distance.

### Save

Click **Save Changes** at the bottom.

✅ **Done!**

---

## 📺 Display Screens tab

This is where you write the messages staff see when something's not quite right on the TV.

There are three screens you can customise:

### Loading screen
Shown for the first second or two when a TV is opening a board.

- **Title:** default is "Loading board…"
- **Subtitle:** default is "Please wait a moment"

### Board not available
Shown when a TV can't reach the board (offline, wrong URL, etc.).

- **Title:** default is "Board not available"
- **Subtitle:** default is "Please check the board URL or contact your administrator."

> 💡 **Tip:** Change the subtitle to include your own contact — for example "Call ext. 4422 for IT support". That way whoever sees it knows exactly what to do.

### No notices yet
Shown when a board is working but has no content to display.

- **Title:** default is "No notices yet"
- **Subtitle:** default is "Notices will appear here once published."

### The "Show our logo on these screens" tickbox

Tick this if you want your logo on all three screens. Untick to hide it. (The colour and text still apply either way.)

### Live preview

On the right side of the screen, there's a tiny mock TV. Use the buttons above it to switch between **Loading**, **Unavailable**, and **No notices** views. The preview updates as you type so you can see what staff will see.

### Save

Click **Save Display Screens**.

If you ever want to start over, click **Reset to default text** — it puts everything back to the original wording.

## 🔤 Fonts tab

Most companies don't need this. Skip unless your brand uses a specific font.

### Adding a Google Font

1. Go to [fonts.google.com](https://fonts.google.com) on a new tab.
2. Find the font you want. Click on it.
3. Click "Get font" → "Get embed code".
4. Copy the long URL that starts with `https://fonts.googleapis.com/...`.
5. Back in NoticeBoard:
   - **Font name** — type the exact font name (e.g. `Manrope`).
   - **URL** — paste what you just copied.
   - Click **+ Add font**.
6. The font now appears in the **Brand → Font Family** dropdown.

### Adding a custom font file

If your designer gave you a `.woff2` or `.ttf` file:

1. Upload it somewhere your network can reach (your IT can help).
2. Use the same form, but instead of a Google Fonts URL, paste the file's URL.

### Save

Click **Save Fonts**.

---

## Make sure everything looks right

Open one of your boards on a TV (or in a new browser tab using the display URL). Things to check:

- Logo shows in the corner of the loading/error screens.
- Buttons and accent colours are *your* colours.
- The font looks right (if you added a custom one).
- The "Board not available" message has your support contact.

If something looks wrong, go back to **Settings** and adjust. Each section has its own **Save** button — click the right one for the tab you're on.

---

## Common questions

### My logo is huge / small / blurry

NoticeBoard fits the logo automatically, but if you uploaded a tiny picture it'll look blurry zoomed in. Re-export the logo at 200 × 200 pixels or larger, and re-upload.

### Can I have different colours for different boards?

Not at the moment. Brand colours are company-wide. (Most companies prefer this anyway — it keeps everything consistent.)

### I clicked "Reset to default text" by mistake

Just type your messages back in. Or don't save — refresh the page and your last saved version returns.

### Where do these messages actually appear?

They appear on TVs and tablets, not in the admin app where you're editing them. To see them for real, open the board's display URL on a different device.
