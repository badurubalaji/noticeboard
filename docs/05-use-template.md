# 📄 Fill in a ready-made template

⏱️ This takes about 1 minute.

Templates are designs that someone (usually your admin or designer) created once. You fill in the blanks — the layout, colours, and style are already decided.

## When to use this guide

✅ Someone made a "Safety Notice" template and you need to put up today's message.
✅ You want every announcement to look the same, without designing it each time.

❌ You want to make a brand-new design → ask your admin to set up a template.

---

## Two ways templates show up

### Way 1: As a widget on a board

A template widget on a board shows the template's design with whatever data is filled into it. Updating it is just a matter of changing the values.

### Way 2: As a notice you create

When you click **Notices → + New notice**, you can pick a template, fill in the blanks, and a finished notice is created.

We'll cover both.

---

## Filling in a template widget

### 1. Open the board

**Boards → click the board → Design.**

### 2. Click the template widget

It usually shows up as a styled card on the page. Click it. The right panel opens.

### 3. Update the values

Scroll down in the right panel to **Template Config**. You'll see a list of fields like:

```
Title:      [ Safety Tip of the Day        ]
Subtitle:   [ Always wear your hard hat    ]
Author:     [ Maintenance Team             ]
```

Type new values. The widget on the page updates as you type.

### 4. Save

Click **Save** at the top right. The TV updates within seconds.

✅ **Done!**

---

## Creating a notice from a template

### 1. Go to Notices

Click **Notices** in the left menu, then **+ New notice**.

### 2. Pick a template (optional)

You'll see a row of template thumbnails. Click one. The fields below change to match that template's blanks.

> 💡 **Skip this** if you want a plain notice — just type the title and content yourself.

### 3. Fill in the blanks

Each template has different fields. Common ones:

- **Title** — main heading.
- **Content** — body text.
- **Image** — pick or upload (see [Replace a picture](./04-replace-image.md)).
- **Date / event details** — for event templates.
- **Severity** / **Priority** — how important is this?

### 4. Pick when it goes live

- **Status: Active** — shows immediately.
- **Status: Scheduled** — shows from a date you choose.
- **End date** — disappear after this date (great for "Christmas Party Friday" — set the end date to Saturday).

### 5. Save

Click **Create**. The notice appears on any board that's set to show this category or these tags.

✅ **Done!**

---

## Common templates you might see

Your admin will have created templates that match your business. Some typical ones:

- 🔔 **Announcement** — title, body, optional image. Good for general news.
- ⚠️ **Safety Alert** — colour-coded by severity. Use for hazards or reminders.
- 🎉 **Event** — date, time, location. Good for parties, training, deadlines.
- 📊 **KPI Card** — big number, label, change indicator. Good for "Today's Output: 142 units (↑12%)".
- 📅 **Schedule** — list of times and activities.

You don't have to know which is which when you start — just look at the thumbnail and pick the one that looks closest to what you want.

## Something went wrong

### The template widget on the board shows "📄 Template not found"

The template was deleted. Ask your admin to either restore it or pick a different template for the widget.

### My values aren't saving

Did you click **Save** at the top right? The fields don't save by themselves — you need to click that button.

### The template's colours/fonts don't match our brand

Templates use the colours and fonts set in **Settings → 🎨 Brand**. Either:

- Change those settings to match your brand (one time setup — see [Add your logo and colours](./06-branding.md)), or
- Ask your admin to update the template's design.
