# 🖼️ Replace a picture on a board

⏱️ This takes about 30 seconds.

A picture on one of your boards is out of date and you want a new one up.

## Step-by-step

### 1. Open the board

- Sign in to NoticeBoard.
- Click **Boards** on the left.
- Click the board with the picture you want to change.
- Click **Design** (top right).

### 2. Click the picture

A panel opens on the right with all its settings.

### 3. Upload the new image

Near the top of the right panel, you'll see a big dashed box that looks like this:

> 📤  **Click to upload or drop an image**
> *PNG, JPG, WebP, GIF or SVG*

Two ways to use it:

- **Click** the box → pick a picture file from your computer.
- **Drag and drop** an image from your desktop straight onto the box.

A spinner appears for a second, then the picture replaces the old one.

### 4. Save

Click **Save** at the top right. Done.

✅ **Done!** The TV updates within a few seconds.

---

## Making the picture fit nicely

After uploading, scroll down in the same right panel to **Fit & Size**. Here are the bits that matter:

### Fit mode

The dropdown has four options written in plain English:

| Option | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Fill widget (crop to fit)** | The picture fills the whole box. If the picture is the wrong shape, some of it gets cut off. |
| **Fit inside (show whole image)** | The whole picture is always visible. There may be empty space on the sides (you can colour this in with the **Background behind image** picker). |
| **Stretch (may distort)** | Forces the picture to exactly fit the box. Use this carefully — it can look squished. |
| **Original size** | Shows the picture at its real size, no resizing. |

### Edge-to-edge tick box

Tick this if you want the picture to go right to the edges of the widget (no border around it). Untick to keep a small gap.

### Width and Height sliders

Drag these to make the picture take up only part of the widget. Good for logos that should be smaller than their box.

### Alignment

If the picture is smaller than its box, where should it sit? Centre, top, bottom, left, or right.

## Other useful things

### Replace with a picture you've used before

In the right panel, scroll to **Pick from media library**. You'll see thumbnails of every image you've ever uploaded. Click one to use it.

### Remove the picture entirely

In the **Or paste an image URL** field, click **Remove image**.

### Use an image from a website

If the picture is online (not on your computer), paste its address into the **Or paste an image URL** box. Most pictures on websites can be right-clicked → "Copy image address".

---

## What kinds of files can I upload?

- **PNG** — most common, good for logos and screenshots.
- **JPG** / **JPEG** — good for photos.
- **WebP** — newer format, good for everything.
- **GIF** — supports animation.
> 💡 SVG isn't supported for security reasons — if you have a logo as SVG, export it as PNG at 600 × 600 pixels and upload that.

Maximum file size: about 50 MB. If yours is bigger, it's probably too big for a TV screen anyway — ask for a smaller version.

---

## Something went wrong

### The upload spinner just keeps spinning

Your connection to the server may have dropped. Refresh the page (F5) and try again.

### "Only image files are allowed"

You selected a file that isn't an image (maybe a PDF or a document). Pick a proper picture file.

### The picture appears but looks blurry or pixelated

The original file is too small. NoticeBoard didn't make it blurry — it was already low quality. Ask for a higher-resolution version.

### The picture is upside down or sideways

That's usually a phone photo with rotation info that some viewers respect and others don't. Open the picture on your computer, rotate it correctly, save it, and re-upload.
